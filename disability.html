Disability Insurance

Disability insurance provides crucial financial protection if an unexpected injury or illness prevents you from working and earning an income. With the right coverage, you can receive up to 70% of your gross income as a monthly benefit, with payments continuing until age 65, depending on the policy.

Benefits of Disability Insurance:
  • Steady income replacement during disability
  • Coverage for medical emergencies & rehabilitation
  • Financial security for you and your family
  • Protection for long-term disabilities
  • 24/7 customer support for assistance anytime
  • Flexible policies for different professions
