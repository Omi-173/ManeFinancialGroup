Super Visa and Visitor Insurance

Comprehensive insurance for long-term visits & tourist stays.

A Super Visa enables parents and grandparents of Canadian citizens or permanent residents to stay in Canada for up to two years at a time, with a validity of up to 10 years. The visa requires proof of financial support from the sponsor and mandatory medical insurance coverage to ensure the applicant’s health during their stay.

Critical Illness Insurance

Coverage for life-threatening conditions like heart attack and cancer.

Critical illness insurance provides a lump sum, tax-free payment if you're diagnosed with a major health condition, such as cancer, heart attack, or stroke. This coverage helps with medical expenses, lifestyle changes, and other financial needs during a challenging recovery period.

Disability Insurance

Protect your income in case of unexpected illness or injury.

Disability insurance is designed to provide financial protection for individuals and their families if they are unable to work due to illness, injury, or disability. It offers income replacement to help cover living expenses and maintain financial stability during periods of incapacitation.
