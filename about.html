Awards & Achievements

2024 Million Dollar Round Table (MDRT)

The Million Dollar Round Table (MDRT) is a prestigious, global, independent association that recognizes the top financial professionals in life insurance and financial services. With over 35,000 members from 476 companies across 76 countries, it represents less than 1% of the industry's most successful experts.

Why MDRT Matters?

Earning MDRT status means a financial professional has achieved exceptional performance, client service, and ethical conduct. It’s a badge of trust, excellence, and industry leadership.

Insurance Depot Ltd. Business Development Qualifier

The Insurance Depot Ltd. Business Development Qualifier Award recognizes professionals in insurance and financial services for outstanding client acquisition and performance.

What does this mean?

This award enhances credibility and trust, proving the recipient’s strong track record of success, excellence, and professionalism in the field.

Vice President's Award - Continuous Production Bonus

iA Financial Group's Vice President’s Award recognizes professionals for consistently achieving production bonus qualifiers over multiple years, highlighting their dedication, outstanding performance, and long-term success in the industry. This prestigious honor celebrates those who have demonstrated exceptional commitment and leadership in their field.

Why is this award special?

Receiving this award signifies outstanding consistency, dedication, and high performance in the financial services industry.

Proud Recipient of The Vice President’s Award – iA Premier Advisor

The Vice President’s Award by iA Financial Group is a distinguished honor given to top financial professionals for their excellence, leadership, and dedication. It recognizes individuals who qualify for production bonuses over multiple years, showcasing their high standards, client trust, and long-term success.

Why is this award special?

Receiving this award signifies my unwavering commitment, consistency, and exceptional performance in the financial services sector. It reinforces my passion for excellence and continuous professional growth.

Proud to be iA PREMIER – 2025 Qualified!
