Critical Illness Protection

Critical illness insurance is one of the most valuable coverages you can have today. It provides a lump-sum, tax-free benefit starting from $25,000 or more if you are diagnosed with a serious condition such as cancer, a heart attack, or a stroke.

Benefits of Critical Illness Insurance:
  • Replaces lost income – Covers expenses during recovery.
  • Financial security – Focus on health instead of bills.
  • Tax-free benefit – Receive the full payout.
  • Return of premium option – 100% refund if no claims are made.

With many options available, it's important to choose the right policy. Speak with an experienced agent to receive a personalized quote and understand the benefits that suit your needs.
